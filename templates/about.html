About TumorVision

Our Mission

TumorVision aims to revolutionize the way medical professionals analyze and interpret MRI scans, providing fast, accurate, and reliable tumor detection to improve patient outcomes.

The Technology

TumorVision uses state-of-the-art deep learning algorithms trained on thousands of MRI scans to detect and analyze potential tumors. Our technology can identify tumors with over 95% accuracy and provide detailed information about their location, size, and potential malignancy.

AI Model

  • Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN)
  • Transfer Learning with ResNet50
  • Segmentation with U-Net architecture
  • Continuous learning from new data

Data Security

  • HIPAA compliant data handling
  • End-to-end encryption
  • Secure cloud storage
  • Regular security audits
